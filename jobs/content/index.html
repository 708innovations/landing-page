Find your next job at the Dudes of 708.

Please note this is an unpaid internship.

Level up in-game and offline! The Dudes of 708 brings the power of computers to more and more people around the world, with great software and great hardware.

What You'll Do

  • Create systems for clipping shooter games like VALORANT, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, Fortnite, Team Fortress 2, and Apex Legends
  • Create and edit media into horizontal and video formats from games like Minecraft
  • Coordinate with the marketing team to time posts on platforms like YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, and more
  • Coordinate new strategies for new video game markets like Grand Theft Auto and League of Legends

Qualifications

  • Good communication skills
  • Positive attitude
  • Team player who shows passion in their work
  • Some experience with shooter games or any other popular video game

Preferred Qualifications

  • Some experience using a video editing program like Adobe Premiere Pro or DaVinci Resolve

What You'll Learn

  • Effective ways to create entertaining content
  • Strategies for hitting trending pages

Bonus points if you have experience with TikTok as we’re looking to expand our reach to this platform.

We provide equal opportunities and do not discriminate. If you have any issues or wish for assistance or accomodations, please email us at accomodations@dudesof708.com. Please note as we currently are an unfunded group, all positions are unpaid and for work experience only.
